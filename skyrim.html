Fallout 4

Fallout 4 is an open-world post-apocalyptic role-playing game set in the Commonwealth, a ruined wasteland rebuilt after nuclear war. Players take on the role of the Sole Survivor, leaving Vault 111 to search for their missing child while navigating a dangerous world filled with mutated creatures, rival factions, and moral choices.

Players can explore destroyed cities and abandoned locations, complete quests, and shape the story through their decisions. The game features combat using guns, melee weapons, and stealth, along with skills like crafting, hacking, and lockpicking. A key feature is settlement building, allowing players to create and manage their own communities in the wasteland.

With exploration, customization, and branching storylines, Fallout 4 delivers a survival-focused RPG where every choice impacts the world around you.
